Monument Valley, Arizona

Right on the eastern border of Utah and Arizona, you can find this gorgeous, unique landscape known as Mounument Valley.

Monument Valley Navajo Tribal Park boasts some of the most majestic rock formations. Some of these masterpieces tower at a staggering 1,000 feet!

As you can see, Monument Valley Park has many beatiful natural features. A few of these include..
    -West Mitten Butte
    -East MItten Butte
    -Elephant Butte
    -Camel Butte
    -Merrick Butte
